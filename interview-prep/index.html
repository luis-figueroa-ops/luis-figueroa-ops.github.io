Interview Prep

Prep Smarter

You got the interview. Now win it. Decode the role, build your stories, craft your opening answer, and turn every interview you've ever had into prep intelligence for the next one.

How this tool works ▼

Four connected steps to win the interview — plus a fifth you can use any time to sharpen answers from past experience. Create a session for each role. Your work saves automatically.

01
Decode the Role
Paste the JD. Get the competency map — what the hiring manager is actually scoring underneath each question — and the methodology behind a strong answer. If you have the company website, the tool researches their recent impact too.
02
Build Your Stories
Describe a real work experience. Get it mapped to the competencies of this role and translated into the language that lands — using the SOAR framework with before/after impact framing.
03
Your Opening Answer
Build a role-specific Tell Me About Yourself — past, present, future, connected to what this role is actually solving for. Closes with a question that hands control back to the interviewer.
04
Your Notes
Company research, interviewer names, closing script, follow-up questions — everything that belongs to this specific interview in one place.
05
Questions & Debrief
Use this two ways. Before an interview: pull questions from past interviews for similar roles and get smarter answers ready — because the same questions come up again. After an interview: paste the JD and the questions that were asked to get a role-specific read on what was being scored and where you missed.
Think tank, not a script. Hiring managers go off-JD. Use the competency map to internalize the thinking so you can respond live — not recite a prepared answer.
