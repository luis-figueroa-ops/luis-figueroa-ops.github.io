Interview Prep

Prep Smarter

Decode the job description. Reverse-engineer past interviews. Translate your experience into the language hiring managers are listening for. Build your Tell Me About Yourself.

How this tool works ▼

Five connected modes. Create a session for each role. Your work saves automatically to your browser.

01
Pre-Interview
Paste the JD. Get the competency map — what the hiring manager is actually scoring underneath each question — and the methodology behind a strong answer.
02
Post-Interview
Enter questions that were actually asked. Get a reverse-engineered read on what each one was testing and where your answer could have been stronger.
03
Story Translation
Describe a real work experience. Get it mapped to the competencies of the role and translated into the language and framing that lands with hiring managers — using the SOAR framework.
04
Tell Me About Yourself
Build a structured, role-specific TMAY answer grounded in your actual background. Past, present, and future — connected to the role and closed with a question.
05
Session Notes
Company research, interviewer names, follow-up questions, anything that belongs to this role and this interview.
Think tank, not a script. Hiring managers go off-JD. Use the competency map to internalize the thinking so you can respond live — not recite a prepared answer.
