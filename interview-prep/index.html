How this tool works ▼

This tool helps you prep for interviews through three connected modes that build on each other. Each role you apply for gets its own session — your work saves automatically.

01
Pre-Interview
Paste the job description. Get the competency map — not just what the JD says, but what the hiring manager is actually evaluating underneath each question — and the methodology behind a strong answer for each one.
02
Post-Interview
Enter questions that were actually asked in a past interview. Get a reverse-engineered read on what each question was really testing, what a strong answer looks like, and what most candidates miss.
03
Story Translation
Describe a real experience from your work history. The tool maps it to the competencies of the target role and translates it into the language and framing that lands with hiring managers — using the SOAR framework.
Think tank, not a script. Hiring managers often take conversations off-JD. Use the competency map to internalize the thinking — so you can respond live, not recite a prepared answer.
