How the Scoring Works

This tool uses pattern-based rules to analyze your prompt — no AI, no API call, no waiting. The field badges update as you type to guide your rewrite. They’re a helpful signal to build your understanding of what makes an effective prompt, so you develop the instinct to write better ones every time you sit down with Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI — think of it as a coach pointing at the gaps, not a grade.
