What is WordSense?

Most people learn vocabulary by memorizing definitions. WordSense takes a different approach - the same one your brain uses when you read. Every word is presented inside a real passage, and your job is to figure out what it means from the context around it. No flashcards. No definitions handed to you. Just your brain doing what it does best - reading between the lines.

Why it works

Reading builds vocabulary naturally because words appear in context, not in isolation. WordSense recreates that same experience as a game. Every passage is generated fresh by AI, every word is chosen to match your current level, and every session is short enough to fit in a break but meaningful enough to stick.

How to play

Each round shows you a short passage with one word highlighted. Read the passage, then choose what you think the highlighted word means. The answer is always somewhere in the passage - you just have to find it. Get three right in a row and the difficulty goes up. Miss two and it steps back.
