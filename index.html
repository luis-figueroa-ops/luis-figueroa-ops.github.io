Sales Operations professional at the intersection of process, systems, and AI. This is where I build in public - tools that solve real problems, experiments that document what's possible when operations thinking meets modern AI.

Why I Built This

Every tool here started as a personal pain point during my own career transition after a layoff. I built micro-learning courses to sharpen skills I kept seeing in job descriptions - change management and project coordination, SQL and Power BI alongside Excel I already knew, and Python basics to hold my own in conversations where some experience was expected. Separately, I used AI to help me document years of work I had never formally written down - feeding it the history of what I actually did so it could help me articulate the business process areas I operated in: process management and improvement, change management and adoption, channel and partner operations, and budget and financial operations support - and ultimately the commercial value those contributions created. Because it is almost impossible to pull years of experience out of your head on the fly in a way that lands with a hiring manager unless you have documented it as you went. From those sessions I produced real artifacts - presentations that walk through my work story and completed Power BI dashboards. If you want to see the outcomes and the work behind them, visit my Operations & Technology portfolio. These tools are the result of that same process: think-tanks designed to kick-start ideas and help you see angles you had not considered, not final answers. I share them on LinkedIn, including my featured section, because problem-solving should not stop when your employment status does.
